# Sales-Transactions-Analysis  
**Sales Transactions Analysis Project**

---

##  Project Purpose  
This project analyzes retail sales transactions across multiple regions and stores to uncover key business challenges and opportunities. The dataset provides visibility into product performance, regional sales distribution, discount application and customer payment behavior.  

The ultimate goal is to deliver **data-driven recommendations** that improve profitability, strengthen operational efficiency and guide strategic decision-making.

---

##  Objectives (Tools Used)  
- **Databricks**: Perform data cleaning, normalization, and validation to ensure data accuracy and reliability.  
- **SQL Queries**: Generate insights into revenue, product performance, and payment method trends.  
- **Power BI**: Design dashboards that highlight KPIs, trends, and anomalies in an executive-ready format.  
- **Data Storytelling**: Translate findings into actionable recommendations for management.  

---

##  Key Insights  

### Trends Observed  
- **Revenue Growth**: Total revenue across January 2026 is approximately **R672.06**, with consistent daily activity and peaks mid-month and month-end.  
- **Product Demand**: *Sugar 1kg* is the most purchased product by volume (high-frequency, low-value), while *Maize Meal 5kg* is the top revenue driver.   
- **Payment Behavior**: Cash payments dominate (≈36.36%), followed by  and EFT (≈32.23%) and lastly Card (≈31.40%).  

### Regional Performance  
- **KZN**: Highest revenue contribution and the highest transactions made.  
- **Gauteng**: Second best at revenue contribution and transactions made. 
- **Limpopo**: Lower overall revenue and transactions but still does a stable revenue income.

### Product Risks  
- **Least attractive product**: Toothpaste and Soap Bar are lowest in Revenue and in Quantity bought. 

### Customer Behavior (via Payment Method)  
- **Card users**: Smaller but frequent baskets,   
- **Cash users**: Highest revenue, showing preference for convenience.
- **EFT users**: Often bulk buyers (e.g., Rice, Maize Meal), representing high-value customers.  

---

##  Recommendations  

### Pricing & Revenue Strategies  
- Investigate Cooking Oil pricing inconsistencies and enforce supplier contract controls.  
- Focus promotions on high-volume products (*Sugar*, *Toothpaste*) to drive basket size.  
- Protect margins on high-revenue items (*Maize Meal*, *Rice*) by monitoring discount application closely.  

### Customer & Payment Insights  
- Encourage card payments with loyalty rewards to strengthen the dominant channel.  
- Monitor cash-heavy stores for potential fraud or operational leakage.  
- Target bulk-buying EFT customers with tailored offers and discounts.
